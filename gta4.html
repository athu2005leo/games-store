Grand Theft Auto IV

GTA IV is an open world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar Games. Grand Theft Auto IV (2008) is an action-adventure game by Rockstar Games set in a highly detailed, satirical rendition of New York City, known as Liberty City. Players control Niko Bellic, an Eastern European war veteran seeking to escape his past and achieve the American Dream.Core DetailsDeveloper/Publisher: Rockstar North / Rockstar GamesPlatforms: PC, PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, Xbox One (via backward compatibility)Release Date: April 29, 2008 (Consoles) | December 2, 2008 (PC)Game Engine: Rockstar Advanced Game Engine (RAGE) featuring advanced Euphoria physics for highly realistic movement and ragdoll effects.Story & SettingThe single-player narrative focuses on Niko Bellic’s arrival in Liberty City to join his cousin, Roman. However, Niko soon finds himself entangled with loan sharks, the mob, and criminal organizations. Unlike earlier titles, GTA IV introduces a choice-based narrative where player decisions alter the storyline and result in differing moral consequences.Key ExpansionsThe game received two critically acclaimed episodic expansions that interlock with the main storyline:The Lost and Damned: Follows Johnny Klebitz, a member of The Lost motorcycle club.The Ballad of Gay Tony: Follows Luis Lopez, a bodyguard and business associate of nightclub mogul Tony Prince.Noteworthy Gameplay ElementsEnhanced Physics: Unlike prior entries in the series, the world is highly interactive; vehicles handle more realistically, and environmental collisions or gunshots yield fluid, dynamic character movements.No Modding/Tuning: Unlike GTA: San Andreas, standard car customization is absent, though players can store vehicles in reserved spots.Activities & Friends: Niko can maintain relationships with friends (like Roman, Little Jacob, or Brucie), taking them bowling, drinking, or to strip clubs to unlock special perks.Multiplayer: Features a robust online mode with various competitive and cooperative modes.
