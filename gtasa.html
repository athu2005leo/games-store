Grand Theft Auto San Andreas

GTA San Andreas is an open world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar Games. Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas is a 2004 open-world action-adventure game by Rockstar Games. Set in 1992, players control Carl "CJ" Johnson, who returns to Los Santos after his mother's murder. He must rebuild his gang, battle rival cartels, and evade corrupt cops across three massive fictional cities.SettingThe game takes place in the fictional U.S. state of San Andreas, which is inspired by California and Nevada. It features three main metropolitan cities, each with distinct environments and cultures:Los Santos: Based on Los Angeles, a sprawling city of glamour, film, and bitter gang warfare in poverty-stricken neighborhoods.San Fierro: Based on San Francisco, featuring hilly streets, cable cars, and a distinct bay area aesthetic.Las Venturas: Based on Las Vegas, a desert oasis defined by bright lights, massive casinos, and organized crime.The areas between these cities feature vast expanses of rural countryside, forests, mountains, and deserts.StorylineThe narrative kicks off when Carl "CJ" Johnson returns to Los Santos from Liberty City for his mother's funeral. Upon arrival, he finds his family fractured and his old gang, the Grove Street Families, losing power to rival gangs like the Ballas. To make matters worse, CJ is framed for homicide by a group of corrupt LSPD officers (known as C.R.A.S.H.), forcing him on a cross-state journey to clear his name, avenge his mother, and take back control of the streets.GameplaySan Andreas expanded massively on the formulas established by its predecessors:RPG Elements: Players can customize CJ's appearance by buying clothes, getting tattoos, and changing hairstyles. CJ's physical stats (like muscle, fat, and stamina) change based on what he eats and how frequently he exercises at gyms.Vehicles: Beyond standard cars and motorcycles, the game features a massive variety of drivable vehicles, including bicycles, boats, helicopters, planes, and tanks.Activities: Players can engage in various side-activities, such as street racing, burglary, gambling, and participating in dating mini-games.Gang Warfare: Players can recruit gang members and engage in turf wars to capture rival territories across Los Santos.
